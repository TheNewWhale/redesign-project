About this project

I started with street photography which eventually lead to me taking official portraits of individuals for fun. Which eventually led to people telling me to take their pictures for money.

I've noticed that portraiture is a very interesting photographical medium given that you are showing how the person desires to be captured.

Relevant Skills & Courses Taken

Photography

I've been doing photography professionally for 2 years now, and have also gone to school to learn all the technical aspects of photography.

Lightroom

I've been editing photos for as long as I've been taking them, so don't worry I'll make sure your skin looks normal.

Photoshop

Do you want to look 30 years younger? Well I can't do miracles but I can remove any distractions away from an image.

Studio Portraiture

I took this college course to learn about how lighting affects skintones and how to maintain proper skintones.

Communication

This line of work requires me to be able to communicate effectively, but if anything I enjoy meeting new people and being a part of their life to capture their next big moment.
