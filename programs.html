Programs we provide

In each of these programs, we strive to embody the love and compassion of Christ, meeting people where they are and offering practical support, spiritual nourishment, and a sense of belonging.

Through these initiatives, we hope to make a tangible difference in the lives of those we serve and to bear witness to God's transformative love in our community.

Holiday Free Meals

During special holidays like Thanksgiving and Christmas, we come together as a community to share not just food, but also fellowship and the joy of the season.

Food and Word

Our Food and Word program is a unique blend of nourishment for both the body and the soul. Not only do we provide nutritious meals to those in need, but we also offer spiritual nourishment through the sharing of God's Word.

Laundry Load Day

Laundry Load Day is a practical expression of our commitment to serving the needs of the whole person.
