Multimedia District Coordinator

This is a collection my work with as the Multimedia Coordinator which include a various types of photography

About this project

Being able to work with a team and help and guide in a lot of multimediums has been a great privilege. Working and collaboration with many skilled individuals has only sharpened my skills. When it comes to helping lead others with projections, sound, graphic design, photography, videography, and livestream has taught me so much. This is a collection of work I've done in the district.

Skills used for this project

Photography

Before street photography I started taking pictures at church, and 3 years later I'm still as passionate as I was when I started.

Lightroom

With pictures comes editing them and having them look presentable for everyone to see, so lightroom is very necessary.

Photoshop

Photoshop is necessary in order to make graphic designs, also to edit photos to remove things that can be too distracting for the viewer.

Versatility

Working with a team that comes from all walks of life is such a privilege given that we are very versatile in our skill set and the vision.

Creativity

I generally am not the most creative person but being able to talk with individuals, and learn in a team setting it has taught me alot about what people like to see.

Communication

This line of work requires me to be able to communicate effectively, but if anything I enjoy meeting new people and being a part of their life to capture their next big moment.
