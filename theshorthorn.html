The Shorthorn

This is a collection of my work done at The Shorthorn

About this project

Working for a publication is something you have to experience to understand, the pacing, expectation and standard is something that is never overlooked. This is a project that is still on going because I feel like I'm barely scratching the surface of what I've been able to experience at The Shorthorn.

Relevant Skills & Courses Taken

Photography

As a photojournalist taking pictures is extremly important, and also if you want to teach something you have to know what you're talking about.

Lightroom

Editing all the images for print and or web purposes is a very important skill to have when working with thousands of photos.

Team Player

When working with a small team of photographers and a large teamm of editors and staff being able to be humble and just learn is a very important skill.

Versatility

Every location and assignment can vary drastically. So being able to dress, and act the part is vital to capturing great photos.

Communication

This line of work requires me to be able to communicate effectively, but if anything I enjoy meeting new people so that makes it easy.
