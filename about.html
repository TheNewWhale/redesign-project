About us

What we stand for

Caring Compassionate Hearts & Helping Hands, Inc. (2C3H), exits to serve the unhoused, unsheltered and the less fortunate. We believe ministry and service begins in the heart. Our mantra is “Feel a Need… Fill a Need. We simply exist to share the message of Jesus Christ one full belly at a time.

We stand on the scripture in Matthew 9:36…When he saw the crowds, he had compassion on them, because they were harassed and helpless…

What we do

Active in local and international missions.
Supports those facing spiritual, emotional, and physical challenges.
Run feeding programs and aid a special needs home in Kenya.
Donated wheelchairs and serves cities like Dallas and Fort Worth.

Achievements

Community driven with local sponsors
Weekly provision of 200-400 meals to the homeless.
Supplies essential items like food, water, and hygiene products.
Collaborates with non-profits and donors to amplify impact.
